Cancel task | 30 | Skips the player's current assignment without penalties. The next assignment will gain the player normal task progression and Slayer points. However, if a current task is cancelled by accepting a new task from Turael or Spria, task streak will reset and the player will need to complete four assignments before they can start earning points again.
This is only worth buying if tasks are from Chaeldar, Sumona, Duradel, Kuradal, Morvran or Laniakea, unless you are near a 10th or 50th task. It would take a minimum of two tasks from Laniakea, Duradel or Kuradal and three from Chaeldar or Sumona to regain the points used in buying this service.
Block task | 100 | Skips the player's current assignment without penalties and permanently removes the assignment from the player's task list - the player will not be assigned that monster again. There is a current maximum limit of 8 types of assignments that can be cancelled - one for every 50 quest points, up to a maximum of 400 quest points. Cancelling the removal of monsters allows the monster to be assigned again, but does not refund points spent removing the monster. Blocking a cluster task from Laniakea does not prevent the individual subset task from being rolled, for example blocking Creatures of Daemonheim can still roll Kal'gerion demon or Stalker creature tasks.
Extend task | 30 | Extends the player's number of monsters to kill on that task by 20% (e.g., 180 Nihil would be 216 instead). While this can be used at any point during the task, the 20% is still based on the initial number of monsters. It does not provide extra slayer points, compared to boss slayer. You cannot extend a task that is the same as your previous task, if the previous task was extended.
Prefer task | 100 | Extends the player's current assignment automatically and approximately doubles the chance for the assignment to appear as a task in the future.[1] There is a current maximum limit of 8 types of assignments that can be preferred - one for every 50 quest points, up to a maximum of 400 quest points. Cancelling a preferred task does not refund points spent preferring the monster.
